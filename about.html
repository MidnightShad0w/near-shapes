ABOUT THE PROJECT

What we are building and currently working on?

  • 1 Part: Online NFT gallery

    Our project will allow each holder of a NEAR Shapes NFT to be able to set up their own gallery with NFT pieces that they own. Each gallery will vary in size depending on the rarity of the NFT. The fun doesn’t stop there, each holder will be able to invite friends and customize their space to their desire.

  • 2 Part: Auction house

    Our main idea is to create a realistic auction house where the creators of NEAR/SOL/ETH NFT collections and other creators of outside platforms are able to gather and collaborate.

Our Vision

What is the vision? We at NEAR Shapes are working on the sublimation of the gallery and the auction house. The main idea is to combine an online auction house in its usual sense and a virtual gallery, where users of our site can truly plunge into the world of art, artists and collectors, and the world of auctions.

We believe that a 3D gallery with the possibility of direct participation in the auction, without any borders, will help you get as close as possible to the Metaverse and feel comfortable. Just like if you were to put on your best outfit, take your soulmate by the arm and come to a magnificent art-event.

So how does it work? Let’s take a look at a specific example. I, as an artist, want to put my work up for auction. In order to start, everything happens as usual, the moment of mint and list. It is after these two actions that everything changes. Thanks to the 3D space integrated directly into the website, I can hang my art somewhere in my gallery and I can give anyone the opportunity to see it. A potential collector, entering my space in the gallery, expresses a desire to make the first bid and start the auction. When the bid goes through, then the fun begins. Thanks to moderation and algorithms, under certain conditions, my work can become the work of the day and appear in the main premises of our auction house. This is where anyone can see what is happening and even raise the bid. Everything is like a real auction! If the artwork gets enough recognition, it can become the art of the week and will appear at the entrance to the 3D gallery for each user of the website.

In fairness to the artists and the collectors, NEAR Shapes will give the opportunity for you to customize your space as you like. As we believe that your expression and your comfort are of utmost importance. Customize and design and share your style with your friends, family, and community members.

Without Borders. Seamless. Realistic.
